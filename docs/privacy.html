1) Scope

This Policy describes how we handle personal data when you use the App, our websites, and support channels. It does not cover third-party services you use (e.g., Intuit QuickBooks Online), which have their own policies.

2) Personal Data We Collect

  • Account & Contact Data. Name, email, company, role, billing contact details.
  • Auth & Connection Data. OAuth tokens/refresh tokens, realm/company ID, scope grants, connection timestamps.
  • QBO Data (read-only unless explicitly stated). Chart of accounts, customers, invoices, sales receipts, payments, products/services, classes/locations, and related metadata necessary to generate reports you request.
  • Usage & Device Data. App interactions, logs, IP address, browser/OS metadata, and diagnostics to secure and improve the service.
  • Support Content. Messages, attachments, and context you send to support.

Sensitive data: We do not seek to collect sensitive personal data. Please do not include it unless necessary and permitted by law.

3) Sources of Data

  • You, when you install or use the App, connect QBO, or contact support.
  • Intuit QBO APIs, after you grant access.
  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, error logging, analytics) that help us run the App.

4) How We Use Data

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App (including connecting to QBO per your instructions).
  • Generate reports and outputs you request; display and export them to you.
  • Authenticate, secure, prevent abuse, debug, and resolve incidents.
  • Respond to requests, provide support, and communicate about the App.
  • Analyze and improve features and performance (in aggregate or de-identified form where possible).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, our processing bases include: (a) performance of a contract (to provide the App); (b) legitimate interests (to secure and improve the App); (c) consent (for optional features); and (d) legal obligations.

  • Legal, Safety, and Compliance. If required by law or to protect rights, security, and integrity.
  • Business Transfers. In a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may transfer subject to this Policy.

7) International Transfers

We may process data in the United States and other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) for cross-border transfers.

8) Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, to comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Typical periods:

  • OAuth tokens: stored while your QBO connection remains active; rotated/expired tokens are deleted.
  • Logs/diagnostics: ~[30–180] days unless needed longer for security or legal reasons.
  • Support records: ~[12–24] months.
  • Backups: per rolling backup schedule (~[7–35] days).
